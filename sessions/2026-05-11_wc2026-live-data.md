## Decisions

- **Mock is the default**, not just a CI fallback — keeps dev offline
  and Tim's WC2026_DATA_API_KEY untouched until provider is chosen.
- **Two real-API stubs** instead of one — Tim's gap list called out a
  decision between SportRadar (USD 200/mo) and API-Football (USD 19-39/mo).
  Both adapters are wired; flipping providers is one env var.
- **Settlement bridge is stateful** with `(matchId, settledVersion)`
  idempotency, so SSE replays / SportRadar duplicate `ended` ticks
  don't double-fire bracket re-scoring.
- **SSE handler writes to `reply.raw`** rather than using a Fastify
  plugin, matching the pattern in `apps/mock-producer/src/emitter.ts`.
- **Cache headers** per docs/22: `/v1/upcoming` is `s-maxage=15, swr=60`,
  everything else `no-store`, SSE bypasses cache.
