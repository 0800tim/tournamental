## One-sentence variant

Tournamental is an open-source tournament-prediction platform that lets you predict the FIFA World Cup 2026 with your friends, watch every match in a browser-based 3D renderer, and share platform revenue with the contributors who build it through Drips Network.

## One-paragraph variant (canonical, use this at the end of a feature)

Tournamental is an open-source tournament-prediction platform for the FIFA World Cup 2026 and other major sports tournaments. Every prediction is timestamped before kickoff and signed on-chain, every match plays out in a browser-based 3D renderer, every contributor shares in the platform's revenue through Drips Network. The platform is licensed under Apache 2.0, documentation is licensed under Creative Commons BY 4.0. Headquarters: New Zealand. Live at tournamental.com.

---

## Long-form variant (use for in-depth profiles)

Tournamental is an open-source tournament-prediction platform built around three ideas the closed-source incumbents do not ship: save-don't-lock mechanics (players can change every pick until the match kicks off), VStamp on-chain receipts (every save is timestamped and signed before kickoff, anyone can verify it), and a contributor revenue split through Drips Network (a fixed fraction of platform revenue flows on-chain to whoever shipped the work).

The product launches for the FIFA World Cup 2026 with a 3D match renderer driven by StatsBomb open data, a molecule pyramid visualisation of every player's bracket, path-based syndicates at `play.tournamental.com/s/<slug>`, and a podium share card pipeline that auto-generates on every save. The full codebase is at [github.com/0800tim/tournamental](https://github.com/0800tim/tournamental) under Apache 2.0, the documentation pack is CC-BY 4.0, and the Drips contract that pays contributors is public.

The platform is operated by Tournamental Holdings, registered in New Zealand. There is no venture capital backing, no equity table, no contributor licence agreement.
