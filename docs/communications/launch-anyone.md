Hi {{first_name}},

The 2026 FIFA World Cup&trade; kicks off June 11 at the Estadio Azteca,
Mexico vs South Africa. For six weeks the entire football-watching world
will be glued to a tournament. That's a once-every-four-years window to
do something fun with your customers, your staff, your members, or just
your mates.

I've spent the last few months building [**Tournamental**][site], a
free prediction game for the World Cup. The bit I'm writing to you
about: you can run your own **branded prediction pool** in about a
minute. No code, no signup fee, you keep any entry money if you
charge.

Three things worth knowing:

- **Free for friends-and-family pools, branded for bigger ones.**
  Slap your logo, banner image, and colours on the landing page. Pick
  a prize (store credit, a bottle of something, free coffee for a
  week, bragging rights). Members join via a link or QR code.
- **Players pick every match through the whole tournament**, group
  stage through final. They can change any pick right up to kickoff,
  so people stay engaged for the full six weeks rather than locking
  in once and forgetting.
- **A leaderboard updates after every match.** That's a built-in
  reason to message your group every couple of days ("Sarah's still
  top, Daniel just nailed the Brazil result, here's the standings...").
