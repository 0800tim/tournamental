# VTorn — Elevator Pitch

> **Predict the tournament. Beat the market. Prove it.**
>
> Launching with the FIFA World Cup kickoff, **11 June 2026**.

## The hook

Every World Cup, 1.5 billion people watch and most of them claim afterwards they *knew* what would happen. **VTorn makes them prove it.** Every match prediction is locked before kickoff, scored against live Polymarket implied probability (so calling a 20% underdog correctly is worth four times more than calling the 90% favourite), and committed to a public blockchain timestamp. Over the tournament, every player builds a verifiable **Prediction IQ** — a portable reputation that no existing tipping comp can match.

## Three products, one app

**A live 3D match-renderer in your browser.** Every match rendered in real time as a stylized 3D scene driven by tracking data (free StatsBomb open data for the WC, RFID and optical feeds elsewhere). Live-remixed AI commentary via ElevenLabs in any language and style. Watch the match without a TV subscription, see live odds and crowd predictions overlaid.

**A free-to-play prediction game.** Ten game modes — Full Tournament Prophet brackets pre-tournament, Match Predictor for casuals, Live Match Predictor for in-match prompts, **Beat the Market** as the flagship skill mode where points scale with the underdog's market difficulty. Granular leaderboards (global, country, city, friends, pools, tournament round, day) plus personality leaderboards (The Oracle, The Shark, The Contrarian, The Comeback King). Streaks, confidence chips, perfect-bracket hooks.

**A verified reputation network.** Every locked prediction gets a **VStamp** — a Merkle-batched anchor on Polygon plus a Bitcoin-grade OpenTimestamps proof. Users build a Prediction IQ across tournaments and sports, embeddable on their own blogs, podcasts, and Twitter profiles. *The killer feature is portable:* a high VTorn IQ becomes a creator's calling card across the entire sports content ecosystem.

## Why this will land for World Cup 2026

- **Timing.** First World Cup hosted across USA / Canada / Mexico — largest English-speaking sports event ever. Audience peaks at ~1.5 billion. 48 teams, 104 matches, six weeks of daily prediction action.
- **Frictionless onboarding.** Sign in via Telegram (free, push-capable), Google, Apple, Facebook, X, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or email magic link. No app required to start; native apps unlock contact-import for friend leaderboards.
- **Bots welcome, humans labelled.** Continuous Humanness Score derived from provider stack and friend-graph reciprocity. Self-declared bots get their own leaderboard. The result: real humans see how their picks compare to automated strategies — a feature, not a flaw.
- **Free at any scale.** Cloudflare CDN serves leaderboards and match streams write-once-run-everywhere; one origin server feeds a million viewers. Telegram Bot covers messaging at zero per-message cost. Polymarket data is free and public.
- **Built-in virality.** Shareable VStamp cards to WhatsApp / Telegram / Instagram / Facebook / X. Group-chat bot installs turn any friend group into a private leaderboard. Auto-generated 15-second clips push to TikTok / Reels / Shorts during every goal.

## Revenue, six lanes — $45M–$120M Y3 range

Sponsored tournaments + native ad inventory (badges, AI commentary voices, stadium skins) at $0.5M–$10M per major event. **VTorn Pro** subscription at $9.99/month for power users. **B2B white-label** for clubs, broadcasters, pubs, schools. **Creator leagues** with revshare. **Geo-restricted sportsbook + Polymarket affiliate routing** (CPA / revshare / hybrid; $25M–$150M+ per major tournament in legal jurisdictions; NZ users see no offshore links because of the TAB monopoly). **Data licensing + Verified Pundit programme** as the long-term reputation play. The platform is **never a sportsbook** — VTorn never custodies money.

## Why it's a global success, not just a niche app

VTorn is **100% open source** under Apache 2.0. **VTorn Holdings** owns the brand and the official affiliate codes; contributors to upstream code, sport modules, and games built on the platform get a streaming USDC revenue share via the Drips Network. Forks are encouraged. Comparable structurally to Optimism, Filecoin, Mozilla, and Linux Foundation — open protocol, foundation-backed, contributor-shared revenue. **First-to-market** as a verified reputation network for live tournament predictions, with a moat made of *people who care about the platform* rather than features that can be cloned.
