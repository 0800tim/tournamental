# Football media, podcasts and journalists outreach: 13 Tournamental priority countries

Companion CSV: `football-media-13-countries.csv` (295 entries).

This list targets media properties with audience reach above 10k, complementing `youtube-football-under-10k.csv` which covers the long tail of small creators. Scope is podcasts, YouTube channels above 10k subs, newsletters and Substacks, named football journalists at national outlets, big football Instagram, TikTok and X accounts, and traditional broadcasters and newspapers across all 13 priority markets.

The pitch: free co-branded prediction pool for your audience for FIFA World Cup 2026, your listeners or readers predict every match, leaderboard updates live, you give away a prize you control. Match-day re-engagement equals ad inventory, content angles and live shows. Sponsored or affiliate revenue available on premium tier. Free standard tier.

## Notes on hygiene

- 295 total entries across 13 priority countries.
- 2 entries flagged with `(unverified)` in notes for inferred email patterns: Henry Winter and Caitlin Murray. All others use either a confirmed published address or a `(via contact form / press)` placeholder pointing to the linked outlet.
- 132 rows use a `(via ...)` press placeholder where the outlet does not publish a direct editorial email; 163 rows use a published or verifiable mailbox.
- All audience figures are sourced from the publisher's own reporting, Comscore or SimilarWeb proxies, or platform-public follower counts at the time of compilation. Marked as estimates where the original source is private.
- Skip overlap: the under-10k YouTube list is not duplicated. Where YouTube channels appear here, they are above the 10k threshold and (in most cases) above 100k.
- Country with thin coverage: Portugal (8 entries) is the weakest market and should be expanded in a v2 pass with regional Porto / Lisbon outlets and Benfica-Sporting-Porto club beats.
