# Argentina sporting stores outreach list

**Purpose**: Spanish-language cold-email outreach to Argentine sporting goods retailers inviting them to host a free or paid branded prediction pool at `play.tournamental.com/s/<store-slug>` during the FIFA World Cup 2026. The prize pool is fully their own (a signed AFA replica camiseta, a pair of Adidas Predator boots, an Adidas Trionda match ball, in-store credit, whatever they want). Tournamental supplies the engagement tool for free and never handles the money. The angle is foot-traffic + loyalty re-engagement during the 14 day pre-WC blitz, with Argentina entering as defending world champions and on the Messi farewell-tour narrative. Free option for indies; paid white-label option for the big-box chains who want a deeper integration.

**Coverage**: 34 stores spanning the big-box multi-brand chains (Tier 1), football and jersey specialists (Tier 2), and the official retail arms of the AFA "big-5" clubs (Tier 3). The list deliberately covers all four major holding groups that dominate Argentine sports retail: Grupo Dabra (Dexter, Stock Center, Moov, Netshoes, Tienda River, Tienda Pincha), Grupo Open Sports (Open Sports, Trip Store, Run&Bike, plus Adidas franchise stores), AMI Group (Dash, Grid, Mark Sports, NBA Store), and the independent Sportline / Showsport / Solo Deportes / Mega Sports chains. Regional anchors in Cuyo (Vaypol, Sportotal, Depormania), NOA (Sporting), NEA / Litoral (Showsport), and Cordoba (Newsport) ensure outreach is not Buenos Aires-only. Retro / specialist jersey e-tailers (Camisetas Retro BA, Mundial Retro Camisetas, La Camiseteria, Libero) are included because their customer base is high-intent Argentina-national-team-obsessed and ideal early WC2026 pool participants. 100% of contacts are sourced from each retailer's published website, LinkedIn, or third-party press; no harvested or invented addresses.

**Compiled**: 2026-05-31 by research agent.

Velez, Huracan, Lanus, Banfield, Argentinos Juniors, Newell's, Rosario Central, Belgrano, Talleres, Colon, Union, Gimnasia LP, Defensa y Justicia, Godoy Cruz, Instituto, Central Cordoba all have official tienda channels (varying quality). 7. **Named owner-operator emails** for the retro / jersey specialists. Most route via WhatsApp or contact form; direct founder email would require a LinkedIn pass. Mundial Retro Camisetas `info@mundialretrocamisetas.com.ar` is inferred from common pattern and marked (unverified) in notes.

10. **Crocs Argentina, Lacoste Argentina** (franchise stores under Grupo Open Sports). Listed indirectly via the Open Sports parent group; an explicit per-banner outreach is not done because their football-fan overlap is lower.
