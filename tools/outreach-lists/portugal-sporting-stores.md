# Portugal sporting stores outreach list

**Purpose**: Outreach to Portuguese sporting goods retailers inviting them to sponsor a free, branded prediction pool for the FIFA World Cup 2026 at `play.tournamental.com/s/<store-slug>`. The prize pack is their own stock (a Selecção das Quinas WC2026 home shirt, a pair of Mercurial / Predator boots, a signed Liga Portugal ball, club replica kits, etc.). Non-commercial framing: Tournamental gives the engagement tool free, the store provides the prizes from existing inventory and drives foot traffic + loyalty re-engagement during the WC2026 14-day launch window. Tournamental never handles prize money. Portuguese-language outreach is the default; English fallback for the multinationals.

**Coverage**: 30 entries spread across the big-box chains (Sport Zone, Decathlon, JD, Foot Locker, Sportino), football-only specialists (Fútbol Emotion, Forca Portugal, KELME, Total 90, Shirtsfutebol, Foot-Store, Sport is Good), online camisolas-retro e-tailers, indie sneaker boutiques (Sneakers Delight, Rotation Lisbon), and the club-retail arms of every Liga Portugal Betclic side worth its own pool (SL Benfica, FC Porto, Sporting CP, SC Braga, Vitória SC, Boavista, Marítimo, Rio Ave, Famalicão, Casa Pia, Estoril). The list deliberately skips outdoor-only retailers (Quechua not its own brand, Berg Outdoor, Pro Camping) and golf / ski / hunting-only stores because none carry licensed WC2026 inventory. Tier 2 specialists are weighted high because they convert the fastest. Smaller club-retail arms (Famalicão, Casa Pia, Estoril) carry inferred-pattern email markers and need a LinkedIn pass to firm up named commercial contacts.

**Compiled**: 2026-05-31 by research agent.

**Note on the Portugal football retail layer**: Sonae sold its 29.9% stake in ISRG to JD Sports in October 2023, so the historic Sport Zone parent is now 100% JD Group. Sport Zone (Portugal) and Sprinter (Spain) keep their respective brands but share the JD Iberian ops layer. Sister chains JD Sports + Size? sit alongside in the same group. Intersport withdrew from Portugal in 2021 (four stores absorbed by Sport Zone). This single corporate stack means **one yes from JD Group Iberia commercial unlocks ~105 retail surfaces in Portugal at once** (81 Sport Zone + 12 JD + a handful of Size?).

## Tier 1 - Big-box chains

These five plus Sportino are the highest-reach surfaces in the country but the slowest internal sign-off. The right route is via national marketing or country-manager rather than a single-store manager. Sport Zone + JD Sports together are one corporate decision (JD Group Iberia via ISRG) so write the brief once and route to both.

- **Sport Zone** - 81 stores nationwide; dominant WC2026 retailer in Portugal; sole shareholder JD Group since Oct 2023; sibling brand to JD Sports + Size?; one yes covers ~80% of mall football retail in PT.
- **Decathlon Portugal** - 34 stores + Alfragide Sports Park with real football pitches; Kipsta own-brand boots and balls plus Selecção replicas; the Sports Park makes them ready-made WC2026 activation hosts.
- **JD Sports Portugal** - 12 stores incl. mall anchors (NorteShopping, Colombo, Vasco da Gama, MAR Shopping, UBBO); sibling to Sport Zone under JD Group Iberia; route via the same brief.
- **Foot Locker Portugal** - ~11 stores incl. Rua do Carmo Lisboa flagship; primary route via the EU help center (no PT marketing inbox published).
- **Sportino** - PT-owned mini-chain at Av República, Restauradores and Chiado in Lisboa plus Caldas da Rainha HQ; 100+ brands, mass-tourist + local foot traffic; faster sign-off than Decathlon-scale operators.
- **The Athlete's Foot Portugal** - ~14 mall-anchored stores; Accent Group Iberia portfolio (sister to Hype DC / Platypus / Skechers AU); single brief routes to the whole PT cluster.

## Tier 2 - Football specialists and e-tailers (highest conversion segment)

This is the fastest-converting tier. Every shop on it is football-only or football-led, so the WC2026 pitch lands without re-explaining the audience. Sport is Good is a multi-brand portfolio operator so a single yes there cascades to 20+ sub-stores including Foot-Store and Sneak'In. Forca Portugal sits structurally between Tier 1 and Tier 2 because its 20 stores carry only Selecção + Big 3 + CR7 lines, which is exactly the WC2026 inventory.

- **Forca Portugal** - 20 stores; PT-owned; only Selecção, FC Porto, SL Benfica, Sporting CP and CR7 product; tourist-heavy locations on Rua Augusta / Clérigos.
- **Fútbol Emotion Porto** - football-only specialist at Alameda Shop & Spot; Spanish chain's PT flagship; ideal WC2026 fit.
- **Sport is Good (PT)** - 20-brand French parent (SPACEFOOT SAS) running Foot-Store, Sneak'In, Basket-Center, Training-Fit, etc.; one yes unlocks the whole stack of niche sub-stores.

- **KELME Portugal** (Geração Audaciosa Lda) - PT distributor since 2013; official Kelme kit for Boavista FC + Liga 3 / Campeonato de Portugal clubs; B2B reach into dozens of clubs simultaneously.
- **Total 90** - PT-owned online retro + player-version replica e-tailer with custom printing; WhatsApp primary fast channel; classic indie that says yes fast.
- **Shirtsfutebol** - tiny PT online operator since 2018 specialising in retro Portugal jerseys (1966 Eusébio era, Euro 2004, etc.); collector cult appeal.
- **LojaPortugalFutebol** - PT-owned online; Selecção + Liga Portugal club kits; (unverified) inbox - cross-check via LinkedIn.
- **Liga Portugal Store** (`lojaoficial@ligaportugal.pt`) - the league's official multi-club e-tailer in Porto; one deal cascades to every Liga Portugal Betclic + Meu Super club retail layer at once.
- **Portugal Store / FPF** - the Federação Portuguesa de Futebol's own retail surface; runs the Portugal Legends retro line + every WC2026 home / away / third kit; primary national-team merch funnel.

## Tier 3 - Club-retail arms

The official store fleets of Liga Portugal clubs. SL Benfica + FC Porto + Sporting CP between them represent the bulk of PT football fan retail spend, but the smaller clubs convert faster on a free engagement tool because gatekeeping is lighter. The Big 3 (Benfica / Porto / Sporting) need a commercial-department contact, not a store manager. Mid-table clubs (Braga, Vitória, Boavista, Marítimo) typically have a single commercial inbox that doubles as decision-maker.

- **Benfica Megastore** (Estádio da Luz, 15+ surfaces) - single largest football retail surface in PT; SL Benfica claims ~14m global fans; pair with a Casas do Benfica ping (`casas@slbenfica.pt`) for the 200+ supporter-house cascade.
- **FC Porto Store Dragão** - 5 retail surfaces incl. Dragão flagship + NorteShopping + Arrábida; FC Porto fan base 4m+; (unverified) commercial inbox - via contact form is reliable.
- **Sporting CP Loja Verde** - 5 surfaces incl. Estádio José Alvalade Multisport Building + Centro Colombo + Almada Forum; reigning Liga Portugal champions 2023-24 and 2024-25, carry momentum into WC2026.
- **SC Braga Store** - 2 surfaces (Braga Parque + Estádio Municipal / A Pedreira); city-centre Liberty St store closing Dec 2025; relaunch downtown coming; great moment to anchor a WC2026 pool to the rebrand.
- **Vitória SC Store** - 2 surfaces in Guimarães (Espaço Guimarães + Estádio D Afonso Henriques); multiple departmental inboxes published (geral / comercial / marketing / comunicacao); easy outreach.
- **Boavista FC Loja Axadrezada** - Estádio do Bessa flagship; the iconic chequered shirt is one of PT football's most distinctive replicas; run jointly with KELME Portugal distributor.
- **CS Marítimo Store** - 2 Funchal locations (D Carlos I + Pita); Madeira anchor; under-served island market with low gatekeeping plus the CR7-home-island halo.
- **Rio Ave FC Store** - Vila do Conde Liga Portugal Betclic side; small club, fast yes; (unverified) commercial inbox.
- **FC Famalicão Shop** - V N Famalicão Liga Portugal Betclic side; small operation, ideal low-friction free-tool partnership; (unverified) commercial inbox.
- **Casa Pia AC Loja** - Pina Manique Lisboa; returned to top flight 2022-23; e-comm run jointly with Desportiva.pt; (unverified) inbox - cross-check `geral@casapiaac.pt`.
- **Estoril Praia Store** - Os Canarinhos; Estádio António Coimbra da Mota; coastal Cascais / Estoril catchment is international + tourist-heavy, ideal for an EN / PT bilingual pool; (unverified) inbox - cross-check `geral@estorilpraia.pt`.

## Coverage gaps

Honest list of what is thinner than ideal so a follow-up pass knows where to dig:

1. **Named decision-makers at JD Group Iberia / Sport Zone**. Only the public help center surfaces. LinkedIn search "Sport Zone Marketing Manager" or "ISRG Iberia Brand" should yield a country-marketing head.
2. **Decathlon Portugal Marketing Director**. Group CEO Javier López (March 2025) is public; PT-country Director Regional João Manuel Rodrigues is on LinkedIn but no public marketing inbox; LinkedIn InMail is the route.
3. **Big 3 commercial inboxes** (SL Benfica, FC Porto, Sporting CP). Megastore / loja@ patterns are inferred from PT contact directories and marked (unverified). The commercial / sponsorship desk is the real route, not customer service; LinkedIn search "Sporting CP Commercial Manager" / "FC Porto Sponsorship".
4. **The Athlete's Foot Portugal**. Accent Group Iberia consolidation means TAF PT marketing decisions sit in Madrid / Richmond AU; a named PT cluster head would speed things along.
5. **Mid-table club commercial contacts**. Rio Ave, Famalicão, Casa Pia, Estoril all listed with (unverified) commercial inboxes; quick LinkedIn pass on "Diretor Comercial [Clube]" should land named contacts within 30 minutes per club.
6. **Long-tail indie sneaker boutiques** (Porta 188 Porto, Droplist Porto, Son of a Gun Lisboa). Sampled two for tier 2; another five or six are worth a second pass if the first wave converts well.
7. **Algarve regional independents**. Forca Portugal covers the tourist strip but pure indie sports stores in Faro / Albufeira / Portimão are not surfaced here; worth a Tiendeo / EMlista pass.
8. **Açores football retail**. Not surfaced. Santa Clara CD is the only Liga Portugal Betclic side there; their official store is the natural anchor but not yet researched on this pass.
9. **Diaspora-facing PT football stores in Luxembourg / France / Canada**. The Casas do Benfica Federation is the natural route; worth a separate "Portuguese diaspora retail" pass aligned with the existing portugal-football-clubs.md diaspora section.
10. **Affiliate marketplaces** (Wook, Worten, Fnac, El Corte Inglés Lisboa). Carry replica jerseys via marketplace listings but not football-led; not pursued in this list.
