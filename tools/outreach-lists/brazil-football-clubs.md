# Clubes de futebol do Brasil — outreach list (multilíngue, gratuito)

**Purpose**: Outreach to Brazilian football clubs, state federations, university programmes, futsal clubs, and torcidas organizadas inviting them to spin up a free, branded FIFA World Cup 2026 prediction pool at `play.tournamental.com/s/<club-slug>` — fully in Portuguese. Non-commercial, no money, just bragging rights and platform exposure during the WC build-up.

**Compiled**: 2026-05-28 by research agent (Brazil pass).

**Why this list looks the way it does.** Série A is deliberately excluded — those clubs have agencies and dedicated marketing teams and will not respond to a cold inbound. The sweet spot for our offer is everywhere below that: Série B/C/D clubs, every state's *Divisão de Acesso* or *Série A2/A3/B*, all 27 state federations (the highest-leverage tier — one yes unlocks dozens of affiliated clubs), the futsal pyramid (LNF Elite + LNF Silver), university sport (CBDU + state federations + the big paulista/carioca/mineira atléticas), and the major torcidas organizadas (who effectively *are* the engagement layer at most clubs anyway). The Portuguese-language UI is the unlock here — most "global" prediction games make Brazilian clubs use an English UI, which kills internal adoption. We don't.

**Non-commercial framing matters.** Open every conversation with this: we're a free tool, open source, no fees, no affiliate skim from the club, no ads in the prediction surface, no data sale. The club gets a branded space; we get distribution. That framing is what lets the operator skip past every "comercial" filter and land in the comunicação or marketing inbox directly.

## Top 10 first contacts (prioritise this week)

Hit these first — biggest reach per email. Mostly state federations plus the two torcidas with the deepest activation muscle.

## Coverage gaps (need a second pass before campaign launch)

1. **Per-club direct email** for ~70% of Série D, all of the state lower tiers, and almost all torcidas. We have the club name, state, and (often) website, but the "Fale Conosco" form is the realistic entry point for those. A manual scrape of each `/contato` page would harvest specific marketing emails over time.
2. **All 27 federation comms-director names**. We have the institutional email for ~12 federations; the other 15 just route through the site form. Once one federation engages, ask them for the comms director's direct contact at the others — that's typically how the federation network unlocks.
3. **Smaller state federations (AC / AP / RR / RO / TO / DF)** have minimal web presence; the institutional websites listed are best-guess URLs and should be verified before sending — some federations have moved to Wix or Instagram-only.
4. **State futsal federations** (FGFS in RS, FPFS in SP, FCFS in CE, FFFS in SC etc.) are not yet enumerated — futsal has its own pyramid below LNF that is dense with small clubs in every state.
5. **Women's football** (Brasileirão Feminino A1/A2/A3) is not in this list at all. Excellent next pass — much more receptive to free engagement tools.
6. **State universities (non-federal)**: UNESP, UNICAMP campi outside Campinas, UFABC, UTFPR, IFRN, IFCE etc. — long tail of public-uni atléticas.
7. **Diaspora / regional supporter associations** (Brazilians-in-Japan torcidas, NY-based Flamengo bar groups, Lisbon Cruzeirenses) are uncovered. Could materially expand Portuguese-speaking reach during WC.
8. **Torcidas of NE clubs** (Inferno Coral / Santa Cruz, Fanáticos / Sport, Garra Tricolor / Bahia, etc.) — only major SE+MG torcidas are listed. NE has equally engaged orgs.
9. **Series D Instagram handles** — none captured here; a 30-minute Instagram pass would harvest the @handle for every Série D club and most lower-tier clubs.
10. **Verification pass**: this list was compiled in one 15-minute research window from public sources. Before any cold-email send, run the email-validation pass + verify each federation URL is live (federation sites in Brazil move hosts more often than NZ ones).
