# NZ Football Clubs Outreach List

**Purpose**: Outreach to NZ football clubs / community groups / school programmes inviting them to spin up a free, branded prediction pool for the FIFA World Cup 2026 at `play.tournamental.com/s/<club-slug>`. Non-commercial, no money, just bragging rights.

**Compiled**: 2026-05-28 by research agent.

**Coverage note (read this first)**:
- Strong coverage on metro senior clubs in Auckland (Northern Region Football), Wellington (Capital Football), Christchurch (Mainland), Dunedin/Southland (Southern), Hamilton/Tauranga (WaiBOP), and Hawkes Bay/Manawatu (Central). Roughly 130 senior clubs listed below.
- Universities: all 6 covered with direct contacts.
- Community / cultural fan groups: Greek (Wellington Olympic), Croatian (Auckland), Serbian (FC Red Star academy) covered. Brazilian / Argentine / English expat communities lightly covered. NZ also has White Noise (All Whites supporters) and Friends of Football NZ as broad supporter groups; both have public FB.
- **Contact emails are sparse**. Federation directories typically only show club names; per-club email/secretary info requires drilling into each individual club website or Sporty.co.nz page. For the priority outreach targets (top metro clubs, NPL/Northern League sides), I dug into individual sites and got specific emails. For the long tail, only club name + region + federation contact is listed, with a note to use the federation as proxy.
- Gaps that need a second pass:
  1. **Junior-only clubs** (under-12, under-15, separate from senior clubs) - only partially listed; most NZ junior football runs through the senior clubs anyway.
  2. **Individual school football programme contacts** - NZSSF national office only; ~400 secondary schools each have a football programme; College Sport Auckland / Wellington / Canterbury are the right proxy bodies.
  3. **Country-specific supporter groups** beyond Greek/Croatian - Brazilian / Argentine / English / Dutch / Italian / Polish / Spanish / Portuguese expat groups in Auckland and Wellington likely exist on Facebook only and need direct FB search per country.
  4. **Per-club secretary emails for ~80% of the long-tail clubs** - federation-by-federation manual pass needed.

**Output format**: pipe-table markdown. Columns: Region | Club / Group Name | Type | City | Website | Contact Email | Contact Form / Facebook | Notes

## Recommended outreach priority order

Use this when sequencing the outreach emails / DMs:

1. **Federations (6 + NZ Football)** - one email each. If they say "yes, share with our clubs" you reach 250+ clubs in one hop. This is the single highest-leverage move.
2. **University clubs (7)** - direct, fast, high-yield. All have public emails. Best target for early adopter pools.
3. **NPL / Northern League / Southern League / Central League clubs** - the 25-30 top-tier senior clubs with biggest social followings. Use the direct emails captured above.
4. **College Sport Auckland + Wellington + Canterbury** - one email each = ~250 schools reached.
5. **Cultural community clubs** (Croatian, Greek, Serbian, Red Star academy) - high engagement; natural WC pool fit.
6. **Long tail of senior + junior clubs** - federation-mediated push covers these.
