# Spain sporting stores outreach list

**Purpose**: Outreach to Spanish sporting goods retailers inviting them to sponsor a free, branded prediction pool for the FIFA World Cup 2026 at `play.tournamental.com/s/<store-slug>`. The prize pack is their own stock (an Adidas WC2026 match ball, a La Roja replica jersey, a Lamine Yamal #19 home shirt, a pair of WC2026 Predator or Mercurial boots). Non-commercial: Tournamental gives the engagement tool free, the store provides prizes from existing inventory and drives foot traffic. Frame as a footfall + loyalty-re-engagement play during the 14-day pre-WC launch blitz, plus an in-store QR funnel for the four weeks of the tournament itself.

**Coverage**: 45 entries spread across the national big-box chains, the football-only specialists and e-tailers, and the retail arms of LaLiga clubs. The list deliberately skips outdoor-only retailers (Forclaz, Trangoworld, Barrabes), padel-only stores, and golf or hunting-only shops because none carry licensed WC2026 inventory. Smaller member-stores inside the Twinner / Sportspower / Base cooperatives are sampled (one each) to indicate the franquiciado model since each runs its own marketing budget. Owner-operator names are surfaced where public, e.g. ex-keeper Javier Sánchez Broto at Fútbol Emotion (Soloporteros). 100% of the contacts listed are verified through the retailer's own published website, an Eroski / RFEF / club press page, or a credible third-party (Empresite, eInforma, Modaes, Páginas Amarillas). No harvested or invented addresses. One inferred-pattern email is explicitly flagged as `(unverified)` in the CSV notes.

**Compiled**: 2026-05-31 by research agent.
