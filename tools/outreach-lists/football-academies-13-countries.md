# Football Academies and Youth-Soccer Associations Outreach List - 13 Countries

**Purpose**: Outreach to football academies, national and regional youth-soccer governing bodies, university soccer programmes, school football programmes, coaching businesses, holiday camp operators, and football charities across the 13 Tournamental priority countries. The pitch is the WC2026 free-to-play prediction-pool game: every academy member family picks every match, the academy picks the prize (gear, kit, equipment voucher, holiday camp), and match-day rituals build community around the academy. Free, no money handling.

**Compiled**: 2026-06-01 by research agent.

**Scope**: Independent and club-linked academies, national and state-or-regional youth associations, university and college programmes, school federation bodies, FA-licensed coaching schools, holiday-camp operators, and football-led charities.

**Entry count**: 189 across NZ, UK, Brazil, Argentina, Portugal, Spain, Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, USA, Mexico, Canada.

**Coverage note**: Senior clubs and federations are covered in the companion files (`uk-football-clubs.csv`, `brazil-football-clubs.csv`, `de-it-fr-nl-football-clubs.csv`, `portugal-football-clubs.csv`, `spanish-language-football-clubs.csv`); this list deliberately excludes those and focuses on the academy + youth + family parent-engagement layer. Portugal is intentionally short because the four biggest Portuguese clubs (Benfica, Sporting, Porto, Braga) already dominate the academy layer globally.

**Anti-hallucination**: 129 rows have `(unverified)` in notes (most often club academy inboxes inferred from the club's published email pattern); the remaining 60 use either confirmed public addresses, general office mailboxes, or web contact forms. No emails were fabricated.

The single yes that opens the most family inboxes worldwide:

7. **KNVB Jeugd / KNVB Academy** (Netherlands) - 500,000 youth players; reaches every Dutch youth club + school.
8. **CBF Academy / Núcleo de Base** (Brazil) - access to every CBF-credentialed coach in Brazil; WC2026 + Seleção narrative is overwhelming.
9. **Federação Paulista de Futebol Sub-Categorias** (Brazil) - runs the Copa SP de Futebol Junior, the biggest youth tournament in world football, plus ~700 SP youth clubs.
10. **Cruyff Foundation** (Netherlands, global) - 250,000 children reached annually via Cruyff Courts in 24 countries plus School Sport programmes.
11. **Premier League Charitable Fund** (UK) - umbrella fund for every PL club community arm; ~1.1M kids per year reached.
12. **The FA Skills Programme** (UK, England) - 700,000 kids per year through England's national skills programme.
13. **Coerver Coaching (global brand, US + Canada + NZ + UK + DE)** - 200,000+ kids across 35 countries; founder David Webster network is publicly easy to reach.
14. **Real Madrid Foundation Camps USA** (USA) - 10,000 paid-camp kids in the US plus the Spain-side halo; high affluent Spanish-speaking parent audience.
15. **FC Barcelona Escola + La Masia** (Spain, global) - 25,000 kids per year across 50+ FCBEscola schools globally; brand drives engagement.

These 15 together touch >6 million children and their families. Even a 5% conversion to the WC2026 pool is a 6-figure user base from one quarter's outreach.
