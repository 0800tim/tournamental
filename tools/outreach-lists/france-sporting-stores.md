# France sporting stores outreach list

**Purpose**: Outreach to French sporting goods retailers inviting them to sponsor a free, branded prediction pool for the FIFA World Cup 2026 at `play.tournamental.com/s/<store-slug>`. The prize pool is their own stock (an Adidas WC2026 match ball, a Les Bleus or featured-nation jersey, a couple of pairs of crampons). Non-commercial: Tournamental gives the engagement tool free, the store provides prizes from existing inventory and drives foot traffic. Frame as a foot-traffic + loyalty re-engagement play during the 14-day pre-WC launch blitz. French-language outreach acceptable and preferred for independents and club retail arms.

**Coverage**: 36 entries across the big-box chains, the football-specialist e-tailers, and the official club retail arms of the top Ligue 1 clubs. Decathlon, Intersport, Sport 2000, Go Sport, Foot Locker, JD Sports, Citadium, Snipes, Courir and Sneakers.fr give national big-box reach. Foot.fr, Footkorner, Footland and the FFF official store give pure-play football conversion. Club retail arms (PSG, OM, OL, AS Monaco, LOSC, Lens, Rennes, Nantes, Brest, Strasbourg, Nice, Toulouse, Montpellier) give regional-fanbase activation hooks. The list deliberately skips outdoor-only retailers (Au Vieux Campeur, Decathlon's Quechua-only outlets, Snowleader) and rugby/ski-only specialists because none carry licensed WC2026 inventory at scale.

All contacts listed are verified through the retailer's own published website or a credible third party (Pappers, LinkedIn, press releases). Inferred-pattern emails are flagged "(unverified)" in the notes column. No harvested or invented addresses.

**Compiled**: 2026-05-31 by research agent.
